How it works

From forgotten box to living memory

Every photograph in our collection was once someone's treasure. Here's how our community brings them back to life.

01

Find & Submit

Discover an orphaned photo at an estate sale or antique shop? Upload it to Faces Found with whatever details you know — date found, location, any writing on the back.

02

Browse & Identify

Our community of genealogists, historians, and photo enthusiasts searches for clues — clothing, hairstyles, locations, photographer stamps — to name the faces.

03

Discuss & Connect

Share theories, post findings, and connect with others who might recognize a face. Sometimes a single detail unlocks an entire family history.

04

Preserve & Share

Identified or not, every photo finds a home here. Quality copies are available so these images live on — and if a family is found, they can reclaim what was lost.
