Tips for a great submission

  • Photograph both the front and back of the original — writing, stamps, and studio marks on the back are often the best clues.
  • Scan or photograph in good, even light. Avoid shadows across the subject's face.
  • Note exactly where and when you found the photograph — this is more useful than you might think.
  • Include any text on the photo or its envelope, exactly as written, even if partial.
